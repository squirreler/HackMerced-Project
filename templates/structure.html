What About Mental Health

Mental health has been a big part of everyone's life for a long time. With this resource page, we hope to provide an easy way to access your university's resource page as well as reach out to fellow students.

Mission Statement

The purpose of this resource page is the notice of university students lacking the knowledge and resources to mental health. Many students do not know how to treat mental health and also ways to get help from it. Within this resource page, we are providing many options and resources like access to their schools mental health or wellness site, professional helps, and also a way for them to talk to people online. Our main focus from this page is to spread the knowledge and exposure of mental health on campus so students have access to these places when they need to go them for support.
